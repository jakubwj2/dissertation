\section{Context}

Maths education is a crucial part of everyone lives. It's primary goal is to equip students with a fluency in the fundamentals of mathematics, mathematical reasoning, problem-solving, and to apply mathematical concepts in science and other fields~(\cite{Math_Curriculum_England}). Government reporting suggest that the average class size is around 26.2 pupils~(\cite{Class_Size_England}) and has an average pupil to teacher ratio of 18.0~(\cite{Pupil_Teacher_Ratio_England}). This high ratio inevitably limits the the attention a teacher can provide to each student.

Today, we have automated tools that can provide personalized feedback to each student individually in real time. Tools like Time Table Rock Stars, Sparx Maths, Century Tech, etc provide immediate feedback to the student and generate reports that can be used to alert teachers and parents to misunderstood topics or when a student is falling behind.
